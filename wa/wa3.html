Ears

Cat ears are triangle-shaped and have a light lining of fur on them. They are very soft.

Picture of ears

Paws

Cat paws have natural padding that look like small beans. They are very sensitive so cats can easily feel things.

Picture of paws

Eyes

Cat eyes are very big so they can see in the dark, since cats are nocturnal. Cats do not have very good vision, have cannot see images very well but they can see movement very well.
